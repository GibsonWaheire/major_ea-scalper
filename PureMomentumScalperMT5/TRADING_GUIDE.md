# 📊 ICT Strategy Trading Guide - Quick Reference

## 🎯 Entry Checklist

Before entering a trade, verify ALL conditions:

```
✅ HTF Bias: [ ] Bullish (HH+HL) OR [ ] Bearish (LH+LL)
✅ Order Block: [ ] Valid OB detected (last opposite candle before impulse)
✅ FVG: [ ] Fair Value Gap created (3-candle pattern)
✅ Confluence: [ ] OB overlaps with FVG zone
✅ Retracement: [ ] Price is in OB zone (50-100% of OB range)
✅ Session: [ ] London (8-16 GMT) OR NY (13-21 GMT)
✅ Spread: [ ] ≤ 3.0 pips
✅ OB Status: [ ] Not mitigated
```

**If ANY condition is missing → NO TRADE**

## 🚪 Exit Signals - Decision Tree

### 🟢 TAKE PROFIT (Close Trade)

**Primary TP Targets:**
- ✅ Price reaches **previous high** (for buys) or **previous low** (for sells)
- ✅ Price reaches **equal highs** or **equal lows** (liquidity zones)
- ✅ Trade reaches **minimum 1:2 Risk/Reward** (or your target RR)

**Action**: Let trade close automatically at TP

---

### 🟡 PARTIAL CLOSE (Close 50% Position)

**Warning Signals - Consider Partial Close:**

1. **OB Mitigation Started**
   - Price is closing through Order Block
   - OB is being invalidated
   - **Action**: Close 50%, let 50% run to TP

2. **FVG Getting Filled**
   - Fair Value Gap is being filled
   - Price moving through FVG zone
   - **Action**: Close 50%, protect profits

3. **Opposite Structure Forming**
   - HTF showing signs of reversal
   - New opposite swing forming
   - **Action**: Close 50%, be cautious

4. **Session Ending**
   - Approaching end of London/NY session
   - Low liquidity expected
   - **Action**: Close 50% before session close

**Action**: Manually close 50% position, let 50% run to TP

---

### 🔴 EMERGENCY EXIT (Close Immediately)

**Critical Signals - Close ALL Positions:**

1. **HTF Bias Reversed**
   - Bullish bias → Bearish bias (or vice versa)
   - Clear structure break
   - **Action**: CLOSE IMMEDIATELY

2. **OB Fully Mitigated**
   - Order Block completely invalidated
   - Price closed through OB extreme
   - **Action**: CLOSE IMMEDIATELY

3. **High-Impact News Approaching**
   - USD or JPY major news release
   - Economic calendar shows high impact
   - **Action**: CLOSE IMMEDIATELY (or don't enter)

4. **Spread Spike**
   - Spread widens to > 5 pips
   - Unusual market conditions
   - **Action**: CLOSE IMMEDIATELY

5. **Opposite FVG Created**
   - New FVG in opposite direction
   - Strong reversal signal
   - **Action**: CLOSE IMMEDIATELY

**Action**: Manually close ALL positions immediately

## 🎓 Understanding the Signals

### HTF Bias Signals

**Bullish Bias (HH + HL):**
```
Price makes:
- Higher High (HH) - new peak above previous peak
- Higher Low (HL) - new low above previous low
→ Uptrend confirmed, look for BUY setups
```

**Bearish Bias (LH + LL):**
```
Price makes:
- Lower High (LH) - new peak below previous peak  
- Lower Low (LL) - new low below previous low
→ Downtrend confirmed, look for SELL setups
```

**No Bias:**
```
- Mixed structure
- No clear HH+HL or LH+LL
→ WAIT, do not trade
```

### Order Block Signals

**Valid Order Block:**
- ✅ Last opposite candle before impulse
- ✅ Impulse breaks structure (BOS)
- ✅ Impulse creates FVG
- ✅ OB not yet mitigated

**Invalid Order Block:**
- ❌ Old OB (already used)
- ❌ OB mitigated (price closed through it)
- ❌ No BOS (structure not broken)
- ❌ No FVG created

### FVG Signals

**Bullish FVG:**
```
Candle 1 high < Candle 3 low
→ Gap between candles = imbalance
→ Price likely to fill gap
```

**Bearish FVG:**
```
Candle 1 low > Candle 3 high
→ Gap between candles = imbalance
→ Price likely to fill gap
```

**FVG Status:**
- ✅ **Open**: FVG not filled, still valid
- ⚠️ **Filling**: Price moving through FVG
- ❌ **Filled**: FVG completely filled, invalid

## 💡 Pro Tips

### Best Practices:

1. **Patience is Key**
   - Wait for ALL conditions to align
   - Don't force trades
   - Quality over quantity

2. **Respect Structure**
   - HTF structure is your guide
   - If structure changes, exit
   - Don't fight the trend

3. **Protect Your Capital**
   - Use proper position sizing
   - Never risk more than 2% per day
   - Close losing trades quickly

4. **Session Awareness**
   - Best results: London/NY overlap (13-16 GMT)
   - Avoid Asian session (low liquidity)
   - Close before major news

5. **Liquidity is King**
   - Previous highs/lows are magnets
   - Equal highs/lows are strong targets
   - Price respects these levels

### Common Mistakes to Avoid:

❌ **Trading without HTF bias** - Always wait for clear structure  
❌ **Ignoring OB mitigation** - If OB gets hit, exit  
❌ **Trading during news** - Spread widens, avoid  
❌ **Overtrading** - Wait for quality setups only  
❌ **Ignoring session filter** - Asian session is dangerous  
❌ **Holding through structure breaks** - Exit when bias changes
